Lab 4 - Pseudocoding and Problem-solving

Challenge

In this lab we must pseudocode together to pre-code a game of our choosing. We have to creat a highlevel list of the actions of the game. Then we have separate the highlevel problems into subproblems. From there we will be able to add this to a JavaScript file, as comments.

Problems

For us there weren't really any issues, luckily. Similarly to the lab before this, it was a pretty straightfoward assignment. For me personally the formatting was not what I expected when it came to the "pre code" tag, it was pretty literal in terms of it's alignment. After a few tries I got it to look the way I wanted.

Reflection

Pseudocoding for this lab was something that was very helpful. I can understand why it's an staple practice while working with code. I feel like it also really promotes the teamwork aspect of paired programming. But overall this was a parctice that is very useful and helpful when it come to planning code and will definitly create for a more efficent time doing the real code part!
